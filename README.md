## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a passionate **Computer Science Engineering student** (Class of 2027) at **BNM Institute of Technology, Bengaluru**, driven by a curiosity for building impactful technology. I thrive at the intersection of **AI/ML**, **cybersecurity**, and **full-stack development**.

- 🔭 **Currently working on:** An AI-powered real-time Intrusion Detection & Prevention System (IDS/IPS) using **Scapy**, alongside autonomous AI agents like my **n8n AI Newsletter**.
- 🌱 **Also building:** "Kheyn Cheyn" and "NutriGuard 2.0" — full-stack platforms focused on food tech, regulatory compliance, and nutritional analysis.
- 👯 **Looking to collaborate on:** AI/ML research projects, open-source security tools, and **Hackathons** (currently working on CatSense!).
- 📚 **Research interests:** Adversarial ML, network security, and data-driven decision systems.
- 💬 **Ask me about:** Python, serverless AWS architectures, React/Next.js, or hackathon survival tips!
- ⚡ **Fun fact:** I debug faster with coffee and lo-fi beats.
