## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a passionate **Computer Science Engineering student** (Class of 2027) at **BNM Institute of Technology, Bengaluru**, driven by a curiosity for building impactful technology. I thrive at the intersection of **AI/ML**, **cybersecurity**, and **full-stack development**.

- 🔭 **Currently working on:** An AI-powered real-time Intrusion Detection & Prevention System (IDS/IPS) using **Scapy** — leveraging machine learning to detect and mitigate network threats on the fly.
- 🌱 **Also building:** A full-stack food tech regulatory compliance platform to streamline safety and legal workflows.
- 👯 **Looking to collaborate on:** AI/ML research projects, open-source security tools, and **Hackathons**.
- 🏆 **Hackathon Winner** with a track record of shipping solutions under pressure.
- 📚 **Research interests:** Adversarial ML, network security, and data-driven decision systems.
